Smug Pug in Mug 2

The smug pug
Who was in the mug
Said "I am smug"
Then the pug said "I am in the mug"
The pug was transferred from the Mug
to the jug
And the pug
Was fug
And in a jug
The pug said "I am fug"
And then said "I am in a jug".
The bug
In the rug
Said "I am a bug"
Then also said "I am in a rug"
And "A tug boat is brilliant"
